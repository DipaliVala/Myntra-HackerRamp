Welcome to Myntra Fashion Face-off!

Get ready to unleash your creativity and become a fashion icon with Myntra Fashion Face-off! This is not just any fashion contest—it's the ultimate showdown where your design skills can earn you fame, rewards, and the opportunity to set the latest trends.

How It Works?

  • Submit Your Designs: Show off your unique fashion creations by submitting your designs to our monthly contests. Whether you’re a seasoned designer or a passionate fashion enthusiast, this is your chance to shine.
  • Earn Points and Win Polls: Engage with our vibrant community by participating in polls. Each vote can earn you points, bringing you closer to victory. Rally your friends, family, and followers to support your designs.
  • Monthly Winners: At the end of each month, the design with the highest points wins the coveted title of "Top Designer." But that’s not all—the winning creation gets officially approved by Myntra and the designer receives the first piece for free!

Why Participate?

  • Be a Trendsetter: Your designs could be the next big thing in fashion. Gain recognition and influence as your creations are showcased to Myntra’s massive audience.
  • Stack Up Credits: Every participation earns you credits. Accumulate these to unlock special perks, exclusive discounts, and more opportunities to feature your designs.
  • Connect and Compete: Join a community of like-minded fashion enthusiasts. Share ideas, get inspired, and elevate your design game through friendly competition.
