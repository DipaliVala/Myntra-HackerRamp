Designed By Shapi Leima

Inspiration and Story:

"The strapless dress features a meticulously crafted structured bodice. I aimed for a sleek and tailored look.The bodice provides support and defines the upper part of the dress."

"Purple symbolizes creativity, luxury, and sophisticatio"

Design Details:

"The choice of fabric significantly impacts the overall look and feel of the dress. Consider whether it’s silk, satin, chiffon, or another luxurious material."

"At the waist, there’s a stylish peplum detail. Peplums add volume and flair, creating an hourglass silhouette"

"Shades of purple dominate the design. Lighter tones grace the bodice, while darker hues adorn the skirt and bow."
